About the Restaurant

Our Story

Chéticamp, Cape Breton

Rooted in Acadian Tradition

Nestled in the heart of Chéticamp on Nova Scotia's iconic Cabot Trail, Le Gabriel is a celebration of the region's rich Acadian heritage. For generations, this corner of Cape Breton has been home to fishermen, farmers, and storytellers — and it is their spirit that fills every corner of our dining room.

We bring together the finest local seafood from the cold waters of the Gulf of St. Lawrence, hand-cut steaks, homemade pasta, and freshly baked bread — all prepared with care and served without pretension.

Whether you're a local returning for Sunday supper or a traveller discovering Cape Breton for the first time, we believe a great meal is best shared slowly, with good company and open skies nearby.

What We Stand For

Our Principles

01
Honest Ingredients

We source locally, cook seasonally, and never mask the quality of what Cape Breton's waters and lands provide.

02
Unhurried Hospitality

A meal at Le Gabriel is not rushed. We make space for conversation, for second helpings, and for lingering over dessert.

03
Acadian Pride

Our menu, our music, and our warmth are an expression of the Acadian culture that has shaped this community for centuries.

The Experience

Tavern Atmosphere

Inside Le Gabriel

Warm, Lively, and Unmistakably Local

Step inside to the true Le Gabriel experience: a bustling Acadian tavern with hearty plates, flowing pints, sports memorabilia on the walls, and friendly faces at every table.

From family suppers to weekend gatherings, the energy is always welcoming and full of Cape Breton character.

The Bar Programme

Craft Cocktails

Drinks Made with Intention

Our bar team approaches every drink the way our kitchen approaches every dish — with care and zero shortcuts. Classic cocktails, Maritime spirits, and a full wine list to complement every course.

Live Performances

Live Music

The Soul of Acadian Culture

On select evenings we welcome local Acadian musicians into the dining room. Fiddles, guitars, foot-stomping, and the kind of music passed down through Cape Breton generations — all part of the Le Gabriel experience.

Free live music every night June through October, 6:30–8:30 PM. No cover charge — just show up.

The Trophy Wall

Sports Memorabilia

Champions on Every Wall

Step inside and you'll feel it immediately — Le Gabriel wears its sports pride like a badge. Signed jerseys from local hockey heroes, framed photos of Cape Breton's finest, game-worn gloves, and pucks from unforgettable nights line the walls of our tavern.

It's not just decoration. It's a conversation starter, a piece of local history, and a reminder that great memories are made here — on the ice and at the table.

The Collection

Our Wall of Fame

Signed Hockey Jerseys

Game-worn and autographed jerseys from Cape Breton players who made it to the big leagues — each one tells a story.

Boxing & Local Legends

Framed gloves, fight posters, and signed photographs honouring the toughest competitors to come out of the Maritimes.

Championship Hardware

Trophies, pennants, and plaques from Nova Scotia leagues, provincial tournaments, and community championships over the decades.

Signed Balls & Pucks

A showcase of authenticated memorabilia — baseballs, pucks, and bats carrying the signatures of players fans around here grew up watching.

Vintage Sports Photos

Black-and-white prints and colour photographs capturing moments from Chéticamp's own sporting history, dating back generations.

Local Team Pride

Jerseys and gear from Chéticamp minor hockey, rugby, and soccer — because community sports matter just as much as the pros.

The Billiards Room

Pool Tables

Rack 'Em Up

Pull up a cue and settle in. Le Gabriel's billiards corner is the heart of the tavern — full-size pool tables, good lighting, cold pints within arm's reach, and just enough friendly trash talk to keep things interesting.

Whether it's a casual game between friends or a spirited tournament night, the tables are open to all. Bring your A-game or just your appetite — either way, you're welcome here.

Tables Available Daily Tournament Nights Monthly
Take the Stage

Karaoke Night

Grab the Mic

Think you've got what it takes? Le Gabriel's karaoke nights are the highlight of the week — a packed room, a proper stage, and a crowd that's always ready to cheer you on (or sing along).

From country classics to top-40 bangers, we've got thousands of songs in the rotation. Whether you're a shower singer or a seasoned performer, the mic is waiting.
